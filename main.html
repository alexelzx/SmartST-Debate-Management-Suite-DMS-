System Origins & Development

The SmartST Debate Management Suite was developed to meet the precise, low-latency demands of institutional discourse. Architected by Alexios Elizalde Xirokosta—Information-Communication scholar at the IUT de Toulouse and Chief Executive Officer of the European Fire Prevention & Rural Preservation Organization (EFPRP)—the platform reflects a rigorous commitment to digital utility and procedural fairness.

This suite builds upon the architectural foundation of previous academic ecosystems, notably the MCalC+ SmartST OS. Deployed for the BUT Infocom 2025-2026 cohort, MCalC+ successfully modernized complex organizational logistics by replacing static schedules with a dynamic cloud framework, seamlessly integrating absence tracking, algorithmic grading, and centralized planning.

Today, the SmartST DMS inherits that legacy of optimization, empowering universities and organizations to host large-scale, synchronized forums with absolute executive control and uncompromised elegance.
